Why is Blood Donation Important?

Blood is essential for life and health. It is used in surgeries, cancer treatment, chronic illnesses, and traumatic injuries. There is no substitute for human blood.

  • A single donation can save up to three lives.
  • Ensures a stable supply for emergencies.
  • Donating can offer health benefits to the donor, such as a free health check-up.
